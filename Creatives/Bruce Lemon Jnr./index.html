Creative Spotlight: Bruce Lemon’s Bold Rise
 

The Grind Behind the Glory

Behind every award is a story of sweat, sacrifice, and sleepless nights. Bruce Lemon Jr. didn’t wait for perfect conditions; he built them. From concept to execution, Bruce was hands-on every step of the way, proving that true creative excellence is forged in the fire of persistence. His journey is a masterclass in grit, and his recent win at @DiversenCannes is the result of relentless dedication.
 
 

Bruce Lemon Jr. didn’t just dream, he poured everything into Time Is Money, funding it from his own pocket with no major backing. That’s raw, fearless commitment to his vision. His Best Actor win at Cannes proves his hustle pays off, but the grind doesn’t stop.
Filmmaking is costly; equipment, crew, post-production, and Bruce needs your support to keep creating bold stories that amplify marginalized voices. Don’t just admire his courage; fuel it.

Black Philanthropy Month

Black Philanthropy Month (BPM), today a program of The Women Invested to Save Earth Fund (WISE), culminating in a multinational summit every August, is a global celebration and concerted campaign to elevate African-descent giving and funding equity.

BLACK THEATRE DAY

Black Theatre Day or #BlackTheatreDay (September 17) is an annual day of solidarity and service, to celebrate and support 21st century Black theatres in their ongoing efforts to build sustainable, institutional wealth.
